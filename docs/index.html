Russian News Archive

144,966 broadcast segment descriptions from Russia's largest state broadcaster, scraped from the 1TV API and machine-translated to English. These are short editorial summaries of TV news segments — not full articles — capturing what Channel One chose to cover each day across 26 years. Each entry is classified into one of 10 categories via supervised learning (TF-IDF + logistic regression, 77% CV accuracy).
